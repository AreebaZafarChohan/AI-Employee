# Personal Domain

This folder contains personal life management items (non-business).

## Folder Structure

```
Personal/
├── Tasks/
│   ├── active/          # Currently active personal tasks
│   ├── completed/       # Completed personal tasks
│   └── someday/         # Someday/maybe tasks
├── Calendar/
│   └── events/          # Personal calendar events
├── Health/
│   ├── appointments/    # Medical appointments
│   └── tracking/        # Health tracking logs
└── Finance/
    ├── bills/           # Personal bills
    ├── investments/     # Investment tracking
    └── taxes/           # Tax documents
```

## Domain Classification

The Gold Tier system classifies tasks into two domains:

| Domain | Description | Examples |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| **Personal** | Personal life tasks | Health, family, bills |
| **Business** | Business tasks | Clients, projects, accounting |

## Personal Task Examples

- Doctor appointments
- Bill payments
- Family events
- Personal goals
- Hobby projects

## Privacy

Personal items are kept separate from business items for:
- Better work-life balance
- Privacy during business reviews
- Domain-specific prioritization

## Related Skills

- `domain_classifier` - Classifies tasks as personal or business
- `task_prioritizer` - Prioritizes across domains
- `context_switcher` - Manages context switching between domains
